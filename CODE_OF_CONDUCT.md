# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the Bytery project should remain respectful, technical, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- discuss ideas in good faith
- focus on technical substance
- welcome corrections and review
- be respectful when disagreeing
- help keep documentation and discussion clear

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- hostile, insulting, or abusive language
- deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- bad-faith misrepresentation of another contributor's work
- publication of private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, code, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you encounter unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the project maintainer through the channel listed in `SECURITY.md` or the repository contact information.
